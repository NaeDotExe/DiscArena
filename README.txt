DiscArena
Unity Version : 2020.3.30
https://github.com/NaeDotExe/DiscArena

This game is a replica of the game Disc Arena.

How to play : 

Tap on the zone (depicted as the transparent rectangle) to spawn a disc
THen drag your finger to throw it !

Encountered issues :

- The phone vibrates a little too hard
- The trajectory prediction isn't always accurate
